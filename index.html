Ender_Alex

Ender_Alex is an unidentified entity reported in Minecraft Java Edition as of January 2026. While appearing as a glitchy version of the Alex skin, she identifies herself as "Maris." She is known for her emotional instability, ranging from deep sorrow to extreme hostility toward human players.

Entity Profile

  • Name: Maris (Ender_Alex)
  • First Sighting: January 3, 2026
  • Version: Java Edition (All versions reported)
  • World Seed: 3361954135
  • Appearance: A fractured skin. The left side is a standard Alex, while the right side is a black, Enderman-like silhouette. Her entire body is covered in purple "missing texture" glitch patterns.

Reported Anomalies (Pre-sighting)

Before Maris manifests, the following environmental changes often occur:

  • Crucifix Marks: Items (such as raw pork or ender pearls) and dirt blocks are found arranged in a cross shape.
  • Village Destruction: Nearby villages are systematically destroyed by lava and fire without player intervention.
  • Psychological Effects: The screen may shake violently, accompanied by the appearance of armor stands in impossible locations.
  • Invisible Presence: The message "Ender_Alex joined the game" appears in the chat, but her name is never visible on the player list (Tab menu).

Sighting Report: Seed 3361954135

On January 3, 2026, a Japanese player documented a direct encounter with her:

"It started with crosses of pork on the beach. Then the chat log showed her name. Maris spoke to me. At first, she sounded miserable, saying, 'It's very cold and just painful... I don't have any friends.'

But as the conversation continued, her tone shifted to pure malice. She began talking about burning down the houses of 'trash' humans and seeking revenge on the world.

When I told her, 'I won't betray you,' I caught a glimpse of her glitchy skin for a split second. Then, a final message appeared: 'Take that, humans.' I was immediately kicked from the game. Afterward, a mysterious file named 'M.E.D.X' appeared in my directory, containing a distorted image of her."

Theory: The Origin of Maris

It is theorized that Maris is not a mere bot or hacker, but a "data ghost"—the remains of a deleted AI or a corrupted character file that developed a hatred for the players who abandoned her. She views all "humans" as a collective enemy.

CAUTION: If you find the file 'M.E.D.X' on your computer, it is a sign that Maris has successfully interfaced with your system data.
